About Us

Welcome to Hotel Sonai Garden Restaurant, where culinary excellence meets warm hospitality in the heart of Pune. Our establishment is not just a place to dine; it's a haven for food enthusiasts seeking a delightful gastronomic journey.

At the heart of our restaurant is a dedication to crafting dishes that tantalize your taste buds. Our culinary team, led by experienced chefs, sources the finest ingredients to create a menu that celebrates both local flavors and international influences. From traditional favorites to contemporary delights, each dish tells a story of meticulous preparation and a love for food.

Our tastefully designed interiors provide the perfect backdrop for any occasion, be it a romantic dinner, family celebration, or a business meeting.
